HABEMUS PAPA-CAT

One tiny command and Pawpe Miau fetches Scripture, leads the prayers, and keeps it all purring offline. Here's what's in his bowl.

READ THE WORD

All 73 books of the Catholic canon — including the 7 deuterocanonical ones (Tobit, Judith, Wisdom, Sirach, Baruch, 1 & 2 Maccabees). Full NABRE translation.

$ cath read jn 3

LET US PUUR-RAY

The Our Father, Hail Mary, the Rosary, Divine Mercy Chaplet, Litany of Humility, the prayer to St Michael & more — browse the purr-ayers or summon one by name.

$ cath pray "st michael"

OFFLINE & NO KEYS

No API key, no fuss. Verses are cached in ~/.cathcli after the first fetch, so the Word is with you even when the wifi isn't. Blessed be thy paws.

$ cath verse ps 23:1

SEARCH & SURPRISE

Hunt your cached verses by keyword, or let Pawpe paw a random blessing from a curated pool when you need a little light.

$ cath search mercy
